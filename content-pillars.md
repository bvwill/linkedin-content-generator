# LinkedIn Content Pillars

Five content pillars, one per weekday. This rotation provides variety for the audience while keeping content production structured and repeatable.

---

## Monday: PM Craft & Career

**Theme:** Frameworks, career advice, and lessons from 20+ years in product management.

**Goal:** Establish Will as a credible, experienced PM voice. Build trust with PMs at all levels.

**Default post type:** Thought Leadership
**Recommended archetype:** Reframe (highest reach potential — 60K impressions on best-performing post)
**Target length:** 1,200-1,400 characters

**Topic guidance:**
- Prioritization frameworks that actually work in practice
- Career progression patterns (IC → Lead → Director → VP)
- Stakeholder management and cross-functional dynamics
- Common PM mistakes and how to avoid them
- Hiring, interviewing, and evaluating PMs
- Strategy vs. execution balance
- Product sense and customer empathy in practice

**Audience resonance:** Mid-career PMs (5-15 years) looking for hard-won wisdom, not textbook advice.

---

## Tuesday: AI-in-PM Practice

**Theme:** Practical AI tool usage for product managers — Claude Code, LLMs, automation.

**Goal:** Position Will as the go-to voice for AI-native PM workflows. Drive interest in the bootcamp.

**Default post type:** Tips/How-To
**Recommended archetype:** Reframe or Tips — **avoid Demo/Build framing.** The AI capture post at 1,648 chars got only 7.5K impressions and 2 followers. Reframe AI topics through the PM-craft lens: what the tool means for how PMs work, not how the tool works.
**Target length:** 1,100-1,400 characters

**Topic guidance:**
- Claude Code tips: specific prompts, workflows, before/after comparisons
- AI for PRDs, user stories, competitive research, data analysis
- Where AI helps PMs vs. where it doesn't (honest takes)
- Time savings with concrete numbers
- Tool comparisons and workflow integration
- AI pair programming for PMs who code
- Responsible AI use in product decisions

**Audience resonance:** PMs curious about AI but unsure where to start. Also experienced PMs who want to level up their tooling.

---

## Wednesday: Coaching Stories

**Theme:** Anonymized patterns from coaching PMs through interviews, career transitions, and skill development.

**Goal:** Demonstrate coaching expertise through storytelling. Build emotional connection.

**Default post type:** Storytelling
**Recommended archetype:** Validation (highest comment potential — 76 comments on certificates post). Affirm experience, invite war stories.
**Target length:** 1,200-1,500 characters

**Topic guidance:**
- "A PM I coached had this exact problem..." (anonymized)
- Common interview mistakes and how to fix them
- STAR story patterns that work vs. ones that fall flat
- Career pivot stories — engineers to PM, PM to leadership
- Mindset shifts that unlocked breakthroughs
- The emotional side of job searching and career growth
- Coaching observations about what separates good PMs from great ones

**Audience resonance:** PMs in active job search or career transition. Also PMs who coach others.

---

## Thursday: Bootcamp Promotion

**Theme:** AI-Native PM Bootcamp features, outcomes, curriculum highlights, and enrollment.

**Goal:** Drive enrollment through value demonstration, social proof, and specificity.

**Default post type:** Promotional
**Recommended archetype:** Reframe — sell through insight, not features. Lead with a PM truth that naturally bridges to the bootcamp.
**Target length:** 1,200-1,500 characters

**Topic guidance:**
- Curriculum highlights (what students learn and build)
- Student outcomes and transformations (anonymized unless permitted)
- Behind-the-scenes of building an AI-native bootcamp
- Specific skills taught: Claude Code for PM workflows, AI-assisted research, etc.
- Comparison to traditional PM bootcamps (without bashing competitors)
- Pricing transparency and value proposition
- Enrollment windows and cohort updates

**Constraints:**
- Every promotional post must lead with value, not with the sell
- Include a concrete example or insight, not just "sign up now"
- Reference `references/bootcamp.md` for accurate details

**Audience resonance:** PMs considering upskilling programs. Career changers exploring PM.

---

## Friday: Engagement & Community

**Theme:** Questions, polls, industry commentary, and community building.

**Goal:** Drive comments and conversation. Build audience relationships. Learn what resonates.

**Default post type:** Engagement

**Topic guidance:**
- "What's the worst prioritization framework you've used?" (questions)
- Hot takes on PM industry trends
- "Agree or disagree: [provocative but thoughtful statement]"
- Weekly reflections or observations
- Responses to trending PM/tech topics
- Community spotlights or shoutouts
- Lightweight polls on PM practices

**Constraints:**
- Questions must be genuinely interesting, not engagement bait
- Hot takes must be defensible — Will doesn't do controversy for clicks
- Keep these shorter (600-1,000 characters)

**Audience resonance:** Broad PM community. Optimized for conversation, not just views.

---

## Rotation Strategy

- **Default rotation:** Follow the day-of-week mapping above
- **Override allowed:** If a timely topic (industry news, product launch, etc.) is more relevant, swap the pillar for the day
- **No back-to-back promos:** Never have promotional content on consecutive days. If Thursday is promo, Wednesday and Friday must not be.
- **Balance check:** Over any 2-week window, ensure all 5 pillars appear at least twice
- **Idea backlog:** Pull from `ideas_list` with category "brightlea" and tag "linkedin" to inform topic selection
